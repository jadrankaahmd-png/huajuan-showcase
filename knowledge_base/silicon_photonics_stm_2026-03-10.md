# 硅光子行业爆发：STM 300mm 量产，AI 网络需求加速

**日期：** 2026-03-10 01:14

---

## 📰 原文

Big news for the photonics sector.

$STM just announced high-volume production of its PIC100 silicon photonics platform on 300mm wafers, with capacity expected to quadruple by 2027 and hyperscalers already reserving supply $AMZN (AWS).

That's one of the clearest confirmations yet that AI networking demand is accelerating.

This also puts $TSEM in the spotlight. Tower has been one of the leading SiPho foundries, but most of its production has historically been 200mm, while STM is scaling 300mm which offers lower cost per chip at high volume.

Still, the market is expanding so fast this likely validates the TAM more than it kills Tower's opportunity.

For transceiver makers $LITE $COHR $AAOI $FN, the $STM silicon photonics ramp is mostly positive. More SiPho chip supply removes a bottleneck which means more 800G and 1.6T optical modules can be produced for AI data centers.
